How it Works

The Image Converter is a web-based application that allows users to convert, resize, compress, and add watermarks to images.

Step 1: Select an Image

Select an image file from your computer or drag and drop it into the designated area.

Step 2: Choose Output Format

Choose the desired output format for your image, such as JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, or TIFF.

Step 3: Resize and Compress

Resize your image to a custom size or compress it to reduce the file size.

Step 4: Add Watermark (Optional)

Add a watermark to your image, if desired.

Step 5: Rotate, Flip, and Crop (Optional)

Rotate, flip, or crop your image, if desired.

Step 6: Convert and Download

Click the "Convert Images" button to convert your image and download the result.
