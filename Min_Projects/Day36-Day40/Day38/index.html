Hangman Game
Rules and Regulations
  • The game is a combination of hangman and quiz.
  • The player has to guess a computer-related term by suggesting letters.
  • For each incorrect guess, a part of a hangman figure is drawn.
  • If the player guesses the word correctly before the hangman is complete, they win.
  • After winning or losing, the player is asked a quiz question related to the guessed word.
  • If the player answers the quiz question correctly, they score a point.
  • The game continues until the player chooses to restart or exit.
About

This game is designed to test your vocabulary and knowledge of computer-related terms.

The game is suitable for players of all ages and skill levels.
