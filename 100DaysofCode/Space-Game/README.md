# Space Shooter: Retro Arcade Thrills!

_Experience classic arcade action with Space Shooter!_ This HTML5 game is a retro-style, two-player adventure featuring dynamic levels, power-ups, challenging bosses, and a star-rating system. Play for free in your browser and dive into an exciting space battle.

## About the Game

Space Shooter is a browser-based arcade game developed to showcase various game development concepts in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. It offers an engaging experience with progressive difficulty, a satisfying upgrade system, and the ability to track your performance through star ratings.

## Key Features

Two-Player Co-op: Team up with a friend for double the firepower!
Dynamic Levels:Progress through increasingly challenging levels with scaling enemy counts, HP, and speed.
Star Rating System: Achieve 1, 2, or 3 stars per level based on your score, adding replayability and a challenge.
Level Select Screen: Choose which level to play, see your star progress, and unlock new stages.
Varied Enemies:Encounter different enemy types including 'normal', 'zigzag', 'kamikaze', 'sniper', and 'shooter' enemies that fire back!
Epic Boss Battles:Face off against powerful bosses every few levels with their own attack patterns.
Power-ups: Collect temporary buffs like increased damage, faster fire rate, shields, or health regeneration dropped by defeated enemies.
Player Upgrades (Shop): Spend in-game coins to permanently boost your damage, fire rate, maximum health, and bullet speed.
Screen Shake & Particles: Explosive visuals and tactile feedback for hits and destruction.
Save System:_ Your coins, upgrades, and star progress are automatically saved in your browser.

## How to Play

### Objective

Destroy all enemy ships and bosses to clear levels, earn coins, and achieve the highest star rating possible!

### Controls

*Player 1:_
    Move Left: `ArrowLeft`
    Move Right: `ArrowRight`
    Shoot: `Spacebar`

Player 2:
Move Left: `A`
Move Right: `D`
Shoot: `Enter`

Mobile Controls:_
Move (Player 1): Drag finger on the screen.
Shoot (Player 1): Tap the left half of the screen.
Shoot (Player 2): Tap the right half of the screen.

Game Menus:
Navigate Menus: `ArrowUp` / `ArrowDown`
Confirm / Select: `Enter`
Pause / Unpause: `P`
Open / Exit Shop: `B`
Exit current screen (e.g., Shop, Level Select): `Escape`
Buy Shop Items: `1`, `2`, `3`, `4` (corresponds to item number)

## Development Notes

This game uses plain JavaScript for logic, HTML5 Canvas for rendering, and CSS for styling. Game data (coins, upgrades, stars) is saved using `localStorage` in your browser.
