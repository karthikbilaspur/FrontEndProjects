How to Play 2048

2048 is a single-player sliding puzzle game. Your goal is to combine numbered tiles to create a tile with the value 2048.

  • Use arrow keys or swipe to move tiles.
  • Tiles slide as far as possible in the chosen direction.
  • When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge.
  • Each merge increases your score.
  • A new tile (2 or 4) appears after every move.
  • The game ends when no moves are possible.

Advanced features include undo/redo, AI autoplay, replay system, and multiple difficulty modes.
