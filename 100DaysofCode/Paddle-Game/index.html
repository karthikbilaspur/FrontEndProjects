Ultimate Chaos Pong: God Mode Edition!

How to Play:

Welcome to the most chaotic Pong game you've ever encountered! This isn't your grandma's Pong; it's a mashup of epic proportions with a ton of features designed to keep you on your toes.

Controls:

  • Arrow Up/Down: Move your paddle.
  • Spacebar: Pause/Unpause the game.
  • Z: Dash (move instantly up or down) or Shoot Bullets (if you have no dash cooldown).
  • X: Activate Rage Shot (when Rage meter is full).
  • 1, 2, 3: Select upgrades from the upgrade menu when it appears.
  • Touch (Mobile): Drag your finger on the screen to move your paddle.
  • R (Game Over): Restart the game.

Key Features:

  • Power-ups: Small colored squares will appear randomly. Hit them with the ball for temporary buffs like a bigger paddle, slowed balls, or even spawning multiple balls!
  • Portals: Two purple-outlined circles. The ball can enter one and instantly exit the other side of the arena.
  • Gravity Well: A central cyan circle that pulls the ball towards it, altering its trajectory.
  • Boss Battle: After scoring 5 points, a boss appears on the right side. Hit it with the ball or your bullets to defeat it! Watch out for its health phases.
  • Bullets & Rage: Use 'Z' to shoot! Build up 'Rage' by making combos and hitting the ball. High rage increases bullet power and affects your dash cooldown.
  • Rage Shot: When your 'Rage' meter is full, press 'X' for a super-powered shot that sends the ball flying at extreme speed!
  • Combo System: Keep hitting the ball without the AI scoring to build up your combo. Higher combos can lead to ball mutations!
  • Ball Mutations: Balls can randomly become "Ghost" balls (pass through things?) or "Seeking" balls (gently home in on paddles).
  • Upgrade System: Every 3 points you score, a menu will pop up, letting you choose between powerful permanent upgrades like a bigger paddle, multi-ball, or rage boosts.
  • Slow-motion: Time briefly slows down when the ball is very close to your paddle, giving you precious milliseconds to react.
  • Destructible Walls: The top and bottom walls can be damaged by ball hits. Break them to create new openings and strategies!
  • AI Buffs: The AI isn't left out! It might occasionally get temporary buffs like increased speed or a larger paddle.
  • Arena Effects: Random global modifiers like "Low Gravity," "High Friction," or "Paddle Clamp" will activate, changing the entire game's physics for a short period.
  • Ball Splitting: The boss might sometimes split the ball when hit, adding more chaos!

Survive the chaos, master the mechanics, and defeat the Boss to claim victory!
