 🚀 Project Overview

Welcome to the Ultimate Word Scramble Arena! This is an interactive webbased word puzzle game designed to challenge your vocabulary, test your quick thinking, and provide hours of fun. Play solo in Classic or Timed modes, or compete with a friend in our 2Player mode.
The game features dynamic difficulty adjustments, helpful powerups, and a sleek, responsive design.

 ✨ Features

   Multiple Game Modes:
       Classic Mode: Solve words at your own pace with a life system.
       Timed Challenge (60s): Race against a global timer to score as many points as possible.
       2 Player Mode: Compete headtohead with a friend on the same device.
   Categories & Difficulty:
       Choose from various word categories (Animals, Countries, Programming, Movies, Cricket).
       Select difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) to match your skill.
   Dynamic Word Selection: Words are filtered by category and difficulty, and the game ensures no repetition within a session.
   Smart Scrambling: Ensures the scrambled word is always different from the original.
   Input Flexibility:
       Type your answer directly.
       Drag & Drop Mode: Interactively swap letters to unscramble words with visual feedback.
   PowerUps:
       Reveal Letter: Use earned powerups to reveal one correct letter, helping you solve tricky words (earnable for every 3 words solved).
   Scoring & Lives:
       Earn points for correct answers, with bonus points for quick solves.
       Classic and 2Player modes include a life system.
   Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment: The game subtly adjusts the word timer based on your performance, keeping the challenge engaging.
   Leaderboard: Save and track the top 5 scores locally.
   Audio Control: Toggle sound effects and background music on/off.
   Responsive Design: Enjoy the game seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
   Engaging UI/UX:
       Clean, modern design with gradients and glassmorphism effects.
       Smooth screen transitions and animations.
       Visual feedback for correct/incorrect answers.
       Confetti celebration for game victories!
   Multiplayer "Attack" (Subtle): In 2Player mode, a player's correct answer can subtly affect the opponent's word for the next round.
   Persistent Settings: Your lastused name and game settings are saved locally for convenience.
   SEO & Social Sharing Ready: Meta tags, descriptions, keywords, and Open Graph protocol included for better search visibility and social media sharing.
   Stylish Footer: A dedicated footer for copyright and attribution.
 🛠️ Technologies Used
   HTML5: Structure of the game.
   CSS3: Styling, animations, and responsive design.
   JavaScript (ES6+): Game logic, state management, and DOM manipulation.
   Google Fonts: `Poppins`, `Alfa Slab One`, and `Press Start 2P` for thematic typography.
   JSConfetti (external library): For celebratory confetti effects.
   Mixkit (external audio assets): Sound effects and background music.
 🕹️ How to Play
1.Enter Your Name: Start by typing your name on the home screen.
2.  Choose Your Mode:
       Classic Mode: Solve words and manage your lives.
       Timed Challenge (60s): Get as many words as possible within the time limit.
       2 Player Mode: Take turns solving words with a friend.
3.  Select Category & Difficulty: Pick a word theme and challenge level.
4.  Start Game: Click the "Start Game" button.
5.  Unscramble Words:
       Type Mode: Enter your answer in the input field and click "Check".
       Drag Mode: Click "Toggle Drag Mode" to switch. Drag and drop letters to rearrange them, then click "Check".
6.  Use PowerUps: Click "Reveal Letter" to show one correct letter if you have powerups available.
7.  Next Word: Click "Next" to skip a word (this will cost you a life in Classic/Multiplayer mode).
8.  Game Over: The game ends when you run out of time (Timed) or lives (Classic/Multiplayer).
9 Leaderboard: Check your score against the top players!
