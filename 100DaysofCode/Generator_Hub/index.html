QR Code Generator

Instantly convert text or URLs into scannable QR codes for easy sharing and access.

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store information like website addresses, contact details, or plain text. When scanned with a smartphone camera, the information is quickly read. This generator allows you to input any text or URL, generate the corresponding QR code, and even choose the error correction level for greater reliability. Download your custom QR code for use in print, presentations, or digital sharing.
