To measure your waist circumference:

  • Stand up straight with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Wrap a flexible measuring tape around your natural waistline, which is usually around the narrowest part of your torso.
  • Make sure the tape is level and parallel to the floor.
  • Take a normal breath and exhale.
  • Measure the circumference of your waist to the nearest 0.1 cm.

Watch this video for help: Waist Measurement

To measure your hip circumference:

  • Stand up straight with your feet together.
  • Wrap a flexible measuring tape around the widest part of your hips, usually around the buttocks.
  • Make sure the tape is level and parallel to the floor.
  • Take a normal breath and exhale.
  • Measure the circumference of your hips to the nearest 0.1 cm.
