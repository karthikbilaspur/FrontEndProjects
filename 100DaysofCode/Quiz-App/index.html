Welcome to the Interactive Quiz App, where learning meets fun! This application is designed to challenge your knowledge across diverse subjects and difficulty levels. With a sleek HTML5 structure, responsive CSS3 styling, and dynamic JavaScript, you'll enjoy a seamless and engaging experience.

Key Features:

  • Customizable Quizzes: Select your preferred subject (Math, History, Geography, Science) and difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard) to tailor your learning journey.

  • Engaging Content Carousel: Discover related articles and topics in a dynamic card carousel, offering continuous learning opportunities.
  • Responsive Design: Enjoy a consistent and visually appealing experience across all devices, from desktops to mobile phones.
